About YesYing

We are a professional manufacturer specializing in metal houseware and wire products with over 9 years of experience

YesYing is a leading manufacturer of metal houseware and wire products in China, committed to providing high-quality, innovative and cost-effective products to global customers. Our main products include fruit baskets, bathroom shelves, wine racks, shower chairs, egg baskets and various custom wire products.

With a strong R&D team and strict quality control system, we have obtained BSCI, LFGB and FDA certifications, ensuring our products meet international standards. We offer OEM/ODM services to meet the unique needs of our customers, and our professional sales team with over 10 years of export experience can provide comprehensive trade and technical support.
