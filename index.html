About YesYing

We are a professional manufacturer specializing in metal houseware and wire products with over 9 years of experience

YesYing is a leading manufacturer of metal houseware products located in China. We specialize in the design, production and export of various wire products including fruit baskets, bathroom shelves, wine racks, shower chairs, egg baskets and more.

With BSCI, LFGB and FDA certifications, we ensure our products meet international quality standards. Our factory has advanced production equipment and strict quality control system to guarantee product quality.

We offer OEM and ODM services to meet customized requirements from our customers worldwide. Our mission is to provide high-quality products at competitive prices with excellent customer service.
